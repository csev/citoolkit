Under the <b> Documents </b> tab, you can specify the default document(s) to be retrieved when a URL points to a directory.  You should probably add <b> index.htm</b>, <b> index.html </b> and <b> index.asp</b> to the existing default files.
<p>
It is OK to have more than one default file.  They are checked in order.

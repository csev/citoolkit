Editing Online Files
  • The Process of Editing Online files is as follows:
    • Save the current version of the document onto your computer
    • Open the document with your editor
    • Make the necessary or desired changes
    • Save the updated document
    • Transfer the updated document to your server
    • Check the online version for errors!

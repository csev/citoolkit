Notes for Calendar Administrator

These are accumulated notes for the product. They may or may not be of any use.
  • On startup before any display has come up, you may see a message about "Active X cannot create object". This seldom occurs but it is caused by a failed installation of the file ODBCTool.DLL. This is installed as a part of the Calendar Administrator tool installation. The best thing is to remove and reinstall the administration tool. (4/29/99)
